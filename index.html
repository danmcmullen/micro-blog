Our First Cruise Complete

We finally made it home from our cruise around Italy, Croatia, and Montenegro in the early hours of Sunday morning.

I had hoped to post daily with some photos and a short write up of the day. I did manage to record some notes about each day so but here was never the time to sort through the many, many, photos taken. The on-board Wi-Fi was less than stellar and it was all I could do to make sure the photos were backed up to the cloud. This becomes very important later.

Now that we are back into our regular daily rhythm I will be posting each day over the next couple of weeks to exploretogether.lol, created as a place to keep of record of our travels and upload our photos as we hope to make a habit of getting away more often now that our girls are grown.
